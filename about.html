About Bara Consulting INC

Specialized Oracle and SAP consulting staffing, built on ERP domain expertise.

Our Story

Bara Consulting INC was founded to close the gap between enterprises running Oracle and SAP systems and the specialized consultants who keep those systems running. Headquartered at 860 US Highway 1, Suite 102, New Jersey, we've built our reputation on ERP domain knowledge, responsiveness, and honesty.

We're not a generalist staffing shop — our recruiters understand the difference between EBS and Fusion, ECC and S/4HANA, so the consultants we place are qualified from day one.

Our Mission

To be the go-to Oracle and SAP staffing partner for enterprises that need consultants who can contribute immediately, and the agency consultants trust to represent them well.
